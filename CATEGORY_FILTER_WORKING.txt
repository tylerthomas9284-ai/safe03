💻 JAVASCRIPT FUNCTIONS

filterByCategory(category)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Purpose: Filter articles by selected category

Parameters:
- category (String): "Productivity", "Wellness", "Technology", 
                     "Lifestyle", or "Finance"

What it does:
1. Gets all article cards from DOM
2. Compares each card's data-category attribute
3. Shows matching articles (display: flex)
4. Hides non-matching articles (display: none)
5. Counts visible articles
6. Updates section title with category name + count
7. Smoothly scrolls to articles section

Example:
filterByCategory('Wellness')
→ Shows only Wellness articles
→ Title: "Wellness Articles (4)"

showAllArticles()
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Purpose: Reset filter and show all articles

Parameters: None

What it does:
1. Gets all article cards from DOM
2. Shows all articles (display: flex)
3. Resets section title to "Latest Stories"
4. Smoothly scrolls to articles section

Example:
showAllArticles()
→ Shows all 12 articles
→ Title: "Latest Stories"
